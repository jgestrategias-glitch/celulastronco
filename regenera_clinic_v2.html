Frequently Asked Questions

Stem cell therapy has shown clinical efficacy for a wide range of conditions including osteoarthritis, tendon injuries, spinal disc degeneration, neurological disorders, aesthetic concerns, and sexual dysfunction. During your consultation, Dr. Rivera will assess whether you are a candidate.

The harvesting procedure is minimally invasive and performed under local anesthesia. Most patients report mild discomfort at most. The injection phase uses image guidance for precision and comfort. The entire process typically takes 3–4 hours.

Initial improvements are often noticed within 2–4 weeks. Significant results typically emerge at the 8–12 week mark as cellular regeneration progresses. Peak outcomes are generally achieved by month 3–6, with many patients experiencing lasting benefits for 5+ years.

All protocols at REGENERA are performed in full compliance with FDA guidelines for same-day autologous (your own cells) procedures. We do not use unapproved cell lines or experimental agents. Our protocols are continuously reviewed against current regulatory standards.

Most regenerative medicine procedures are not currently covered by standard insurance plans. However, we offer flexible financing options and work with several medical financing partners to make treatment accessible. Our team will discuss all financial options during your consultation.
